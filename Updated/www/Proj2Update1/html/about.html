About UMBC Degree Advisor

The UMBC Degree Advisor is a student group project conducted for UMBC's Computer Science 433 Scripting Languages course of Spring 2016. The project consists of two phases, with development handed off between two teams.

The goals of the Scripting Languages group project consist of providing planning, coding, and organizational experience, incorporating and reinforcing all covered course material, in addition to producing a useful product. Development is ongoing and changes to the Course Advisor are frequent.
